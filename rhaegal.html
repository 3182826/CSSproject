About

Rhaegal was one of Daenerys Targaryen's three dragons, their eggs given to Daenerys as a wedding gift. Rhaegal
was named after her late elder brother, Rhaegar.

Feats

In the books, Rhaegal attacks the Good Masters and slavers. In the show, Rhaegal assists Daenerys in her battles.

Death

Did not die in the books. Died in the show, shot out of the sky by Euron Greyjoy.
