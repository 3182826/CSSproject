About

Sunfyre, his aliases being 'Sunfyre the Golden' and 'King Aegon's Glory' was a beautiful dragon known for his
beautiful, gleaming gold scales. He is described as 'the most beautiful dragon ever seen in the known world'
by Archmaster Gyldayn. Sunfyre's only rider was Aegon II Targaryen, participating in battles during the
Dance of the Dragons.

Feats

Participated in the battle at Rook's Rest, although suffering with a half-torn injured wing. Sunfyre remained at
Rook's Rest to heal and was then attacked by Walys Mooton. Sunfyre fought back, killing him and a threescore
of his men. Fought and devoured Grey Ghost. Fought with his rider Aegon against Lady Baela Targaryen and her
dragon Moondancer. Although he had slain Moondancer, Sunfyre became more injured than before in the aftermath.
Burned Queen Rhaenyra to death and ate her.

Death

Died after succumbing to his wounds, refusing to eat.
