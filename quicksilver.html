About

A quarter of the size of Balerion, Quicksilver bonded with infant Prince Aenys Targaryen who was also born in 7
AC. Aenys' sickly health greatly improved after they were bonded, this also helped squander the rumours of
Aenys being a bastard. Quicksilver was a beloved dragon to Prince Aenys. When Aenys died in early 42 AC,
Quicksilver lit his funeral pyre. Prince Aegon was her next rider.

Feats

No major battles or feats. Died in the Battle Beneath God's Eye alongside her rider Prince Aegon against Balerion
and King Maegor. Balerion was much larger, Quicksilver not standing a chance.

Death

Quicksilver died alongside her rider Prince Aegon during the Battle Beneath God's Eye against King Maegor Targaryen
and Balerion. Balerion had bitten into Quicksilver's neck and ripped off one of her wings. The dragon and her
rider fell to their deaths.
