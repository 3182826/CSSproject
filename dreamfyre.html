About

Dreamfyre was first the dragon of Princess Rhaena Targaryen, the pair flying together all around Westeros for both
leisure and to assist Rhaena in finding her lost daughter Aerea. When Rhaena eventually passed, years later
Dreamfyre was claimed by Princess/Queen Helaena Targaryen. Helaena was a dragon dreamer who could foretell the
future with her visions. When Helaena died, killing herself after the death of her children, it is said that
Dreamfyre let out a roar and snapped two of the chains that bound her in the dragonpit, sensing her rider's
death.

Feats

While Dreamfyre never participated in any battles, she was mother to multiple clutches of eggs - some speculate
that Danaerys' Dragon, Rhagal, and Viserion were from one of Dreamfyre's clutches (the three eggs stolen from
Princess Rhaena by her female friend many years ago). Dreamfyre also killed many people during the Storming
of the Dragon Pit during the Dance of the Dragons.

Death

Dreamfyre died during the storming of the dragon pit. She had flown to the top of the dragonpit after being shot
in the eye. causing the stone roof to crumble down on her and her human assailants. Her firepower and the
rubble she caused took out a considerable amount of people.
