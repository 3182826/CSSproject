About

Drogon is one of Daenerys Targaryen's three dragons, nicknamed 'Balerion come again,' 'The Winged Shadow,' 'the
Queen's Monster,' 'the Black Beast' and more. Drogon was the largest of the three dragons, known to be the
more aggressive, fiercest, and wildest of his siblings. He is named after Daenerys' late husband Khal Drogo.

Feats

Even as a small young dragon, Drogon assisted Daenerys in attacking the pulsing heart and slaying the Undying
Ones who were attacking Daenerys. The first of her three dragons to fly across the water from ship to ship
and was the first of the three to kill, flaming fish to catch them. Drogon attacks Kraznys' face when
Daenerys tells him that a dragon is not a slave, Drogon then proceeds to attack with the Unsullied. Drogon
sets a fighting arena in Daznak's Pit in Meereen aflame. In the show, Drogon assists Daenerys with her
battles, setting ships, King's Landing, the nNight King's army, and enemy armies aflame. This does not
happen in the books (yet, as the series is unfinished and ongoing.)
