About

Syrax was Rhaenyra Targaryen's dragon since youth. She was well-fed, never hunted for her own meals. She was
huge and formidable, but not experienced in battle at all.

Feats

Did not participate inany battles, but she did birth clutches of dragon eggs and hatchlings.

Death

Syrax died fighting the rioters at the Storming of the Dragonpit during the Dance of the Dragons. She could have
stayed up in the air but instead chose to fight, fire tooth and claw against the rioters who killed the other
dragons. Alas, she was slain.
