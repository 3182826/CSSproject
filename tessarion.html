About

Tessarion was nicknamed 'the Blue Queen,' for her beautiful cobalt blue colour with copper accents. Tessarion
belonged to prince Daeron Targaryen, nicknamed 'Daeron the Daring.' In size, she was described as being one
third the size of Vermithor.

Feats

Battled alongside his rider Daeron in the Dance of the Dragons. She participated in the Battle on the Honeywine,
fought alongisde the Hightower army up Roseroad, the First Battle of Tumbleton, and the Second Battle of
Tumbleton where she died.

Death

Died in the Second Battle of Tumbleton as the riderless Tessarion joined the battle between Seasmoke and Vermithor.
Tessarion's rider died in the beginning of the battle, riderless she rode up to join Seasmoke in the skies.
However, some noted that it looked like Seasmoke and Tessarion were engaged in a mating dance rather than a
battle. When Vermithor joined, it became a battle with all dragons slain.
