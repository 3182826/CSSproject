About

Vhagar is a formidable dragon, growing to be one of the largest dragons in Westeros. It was said that her
roar was so loud that it could shake the foundation of Storm's End. She had survived hundreds of battles.
Vhagar hatched on Dragonstone, claimed by Visenya Targaryen before her marriage to her brother Aegon the
Conqueror. Vhagar remained Visenya's for the entirety of the Targaryen Conquest. When Visenya eventually
passed, that left Vhagar riderless. She was then claimed by Baelon Targaryen during one of the Dornish
wars. When Baelon passed, Vhagar was then claimed by Lady Laena Velaryon. After Laena, she was claimed
by Aemond Targaryen, who would be her last and final rider before death.

Feats

During Aegon's conquest, Vhagar had participated in many battles: taking Stokeworth, burning the ships of the
Arryn fleet in Gulltown, Cracjclaw Point to accept pledges of fealty from the lords, and the field of Fire.
The Trident inthe Riverlands, Queen Sharra Arryn bending the knee at the Vale. All of these battles, Vhagar
did with Visenya as her rider. Vhagar and Visenya fought together in the first Dornish War, assited Visenya
to tname Visenya's son Maegor as king, taking the throne. When Prince Baelon claimed Vhagar, they fought in
battle alongside Caraxes and Vermithor during the Fourth Dornish War. Vhagar participated in no battles when
Laena was her rider. When Prince Aemond Targaryen claimed Vhagar during the Dance of the Dragons, Vhagar
once again fought. Vhagar killed Arrax, assisted in killing Meleys, burnt down the Riverlands with Aemond,
and battled against Prince Daemon Targaryen and Caraxes. This battle was known to be the Battle Above God's
Eye and resulted in both their deaths - rider's and dragons alike.

Death

Died in battle against Daemon and Caraxes at the Battle Above God's Eye during the Dance of the Dragons.
Vhagar and Aemond's corpses remain at the bottom of the sea.
