About

The Cannibal is the largest out of the three known Wild Dragons to reside on Dragonstone, some stating that the
Cannibal was close to Vhagar and Vermithor in size - but not larger than. The Cannibal earned its name by his
actions - being one of the only dragons known to constantly commit acts of cannibalism, feasting on young and
smaller dragons, dead dragons, and dragon eggs. The smallfolk speak of how the Cannibal has been there the
longest out of the wild dragons, some speculating that the Cannibal has been living on Dragonstone before the
Targaryen's even came to Westeros. This lead to theories as to whether or not the Cannibal was a Valyrian
dragon or not, many fans speculating that he is a Westerosi breed of dragon instead.

Feats

The Cannibal was a massive wild dragon, known to commit acts of cannibalism. This intelligent beast resided in
the back of Dragonmont, making its lair there. Not only was this dragon infamous for his cannibalism, but also
with how many people he has killed. Every man that has attempted to tame and claim this dragon has died, his
lair riddled with the bones and carcasses of dragons and humans alike.

Death

It was unknown when or if this dragon even died. No one knows how long a dragon's lifespan could truly be. After
the dance of the dragons, the Cannibal mysteriously vanished, known to be alive yet never to be seen again.
