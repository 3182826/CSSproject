About

Grey Ghost was a pale grey-white wild and unclaimed dragon, the townsfolk naming him as he was the colour of the
morning mist. He was a shy dragon, avoiding men and hiding amidst the mist, clouds, and fog. Grey Ghost was
one of the three wild dragons on Dragonstone alongisde the Cannibal and Sheepstealer and similarly to how
Sheepstealer had a taste for mutton, Grey Ghost had a preference to feed on fish.

Feats

Never claimed despite the attempts of thr dragonseeds and never participated in battles of any kind. Was killed
by another dragon and partially devoured.

Death

Was attacked, seen to be fighting another dragon in the skies. Eventually, he was slain and partially devoured.
Many assumed it to be the work of the Cannibal, but it turned out to be Sunfyre who attacked and partially
ate him.
