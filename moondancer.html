About

Moondancer was Baela Targaryen;s dragon, no larger or heavier than a warhorse. A slender, pale green dragon with
pearl horns, crest, and wingbones. She was a very swift, quick dragon in the skies and on foot.
largest dragon and for his first rider being Aegon the Conqueror.

Feats

Moondancer remained at Dragonstone for the majority of the Dance of the Dragons as she wasnt large enough to ride
or mount yet. Eventually she battled Sunfyre and Aegon with her rider Baela. Moondamcer was swift and nimble
against the larger Sunfyre. Even as Sunfyre slew fire at Moondancer's face, the dragon continued her advances,
slamming into Sunfyre and falling to the ground. Once grounded, Moondancer could not escape Sunfyre, who
devoured her carcass. Baela survived. They lost but was able to mortally injure Sunfyre beyond repair.

Death

Died battling Sunfyre.
