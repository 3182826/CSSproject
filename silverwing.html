About

Silverwing was a she-dragon, first conded with Alysanne sonce birth, egg placed in her cradle. While with
Alysanne, she lived a peaceful life and was frequently seen coiled with Vermithor, Alysanne's husband's
dragon. It was said that they were bonded. When Alysanne died of natural causes, Silverwing remained
riderless until claimed by Ulf White, a bastard.

Feats

Battled with Ulf White. Fought in the Battle of the Gullet, the first battle at Tumbleton, the secon battle of
Tumbleton where it was said she attempted to raise the wings of the deceased Vermithor. She flew away
listlessly across the field. When Ulf WHite was poisoned, she became riderless once again. In her later years
she became a wild dragon, not allowing anyone to claim her again. Silverwing survived the dance of the dragons
and continued living at Red Lake during Aegon III Targaryen's rule.

Death

Most likely died of old age.
