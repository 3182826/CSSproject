About

Seasmoke was a nimb;e dragon, much faster than his older brothers. In size, this pale silver-grey dragon is
around three times smaller than Vermithor. His riders were Ser Laenor Velaryon and Addam of Hull.

Feats

With his rider being Laenor Velaryon, he had participated in aiding Daemon in the Stepstones. Seasmoke was Laenor
Velaryon's pride. After Laenor's death, Seasmoke was riderless for around a decade until he was claimed by
Addam of Hull during the Dance of the Dragons. With Addam of Hull, Laenor's apparent bastard son but rumoured
to be bastard's of Laenor's father Ser Corlys Velaryon, Seasmoke participated in the Battle in the Gullet,
the Fall of King's Landing and the Second Battle of Tumbleton. During the Battle of TUmbleton, Seasmoke died
alongside his rider Addam.

Death

Died in the Second Battle of Tumbleton, throat torn apart by Vermithor.
